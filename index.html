How a Session Works
Fitness & Movement
Fun, age-appropriate movement that builds coordination, confidence, and energy. Exercises are primarily bodyweight and adapted to each child.
Chess & Strategy
Structured chess instruction focused on thinking ahead, problem-solving, patience, and learning to stay calm under pressure.

Our program blends equal parts fitness and chess. If you'd like a session that focuses more on one or the other, just ask and we'll tailor it.

For Parents

Why This Works
Physical activity increases focus, improves mood, and helps kids learn more effectively — which is why combining movement with thinking is so powerful.
